VegStyle is a non-profit Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO) dedicated to accelerating animal advocacy through accessible technology. Our mission is to provide a free, comprehensive platform for the vegan community.

## ğŸ“± The VegStyle App

We are developing an all-in-one mobile application designed to simplify vegan activism and daily lifestyle choices:

- **Petition Hub**: A centralized place to discover, track, and sign petitions for animal rights.
- **Product Scanner**: Scan barcodes to instantly verify the vegan status of products and contribute to a shared database.
- **Ethical News & Events**: Stay updated with the latest news and find local vegan events or activism opportunities.
- **Transparent Donations**: A system to direct donations directly to verified animal sanctuaries and non-profit organizations.
